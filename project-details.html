Level Design

Project Titan

Role: Lead Level Designer
Platform: PC / Console
Engine: Unreal Engine 5
Play Demo on Itch.io

Project Info

  • Duration: 4 Months
  • Team Size: 5 Members
  • My Role: Level Design / Lighting

Tools Used

Unreal Engine 5 Maya Photoshop Perforce

Responsibilities

  • Designed and blocked out the initial layout for the "Central Plaza" map section.
  • Implemented gameplay scripting for dynamic events using Blueprints.
  • Collaborated with Environment Artists to ensure metrics were maintained during art pass.
  • Optimized level streaming and culling for performance targets on console.

Design Process

The goal was to create a highly vertical map that encouraged sniper play while providing ample cover for flankers. I started with a 2D top-down sketch to establish the flow and major choke points.

After the initial blockout, we playtested weekly. Feedback indicated that the central sightline was too dominant, so I added underground tunnels to provide a safer route for close-quarters combat characters.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Navigation Clarity

Players were getting lost in the multi-level vertical sections.

Solution:

I implemented a consistent lighting language—cool blue lights for lower paths and warm orange lights for upper paths—to subconsciously guide player orientation.
